FAQ

Who can come?

Any professional involved in CS or STEM education is welcome.

What is coding&&community?

coding&&community is a group of students at RPI dedicated to encouraging underrepresented students to pursue computer science and STEM fields. We teach weekly STEP lessons as well as hosting outreach events for local students. All of our programs are completely free of charge.

Where is parking?

There is parking right outside of the Heffner Alumni House.

Will there be food?

We will provide coffee, snacks and other light refreshments.
